TextArea Tag

Defines a multi-line text input control.
It is often used in a form, to collect user inputs like comments or reviews.
A text area can hold an unlimited number of characters. The size of a text area is specified by the "cols" and "rows" attributes.
The "name" attribute is needed to reference the form data after the form is submitted.
The "id" attribute is needed to associate the text area with a label.
